# Prescription System - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Minutes

### For Practitioners

#### Step 1: Access Prescription Management
1. Login to your practitioner account
2. Click on the **"Prescriptions"** tab in your dashboard navigation

#### Step 2: View Confirmed Sessions
- You'll see two tabs: **"Confirmed Sessions"** and **"All Prescriptions"**
- The "Confirmed Sessions" tab shows all patients with confirmed or completed bookings
- Look for patients who need prescriptions

#### Step 3: Create a Prescription
1. Click the **"Create Prescription"** button next to a patient's name
2. The patient information will be auto-filled
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Diagnosis**: Enter the patient's condition
   - **Medications**: Add at least one medication with:
     - Medicine name
     - Dosage (e.g., "500mg")
     - Frequency (select from dropdown)
     - Instructions (optional)
   - **Duration**: Select treatment duration (default: 30 days)
   - **Special Instructions**: Any important notes for the patient
   - **Internal Notes**: Your private notes (optional)
4. Click **"Create Prescription"**
5. You'll see a success message!

#### Step 4: Manage Prescriptions
1. Switch to the **"All Prescriptions"** tab
2. Use the search bar to find specific prescriptions
3. Filter by status (Active, Completed, Cancelled)
4. For each prescription, you can:
   - 👁️ **View PDF** - Opens in new browser tab
   - ⬇️ **Download** - Saves PDF to your device

### For Patients

#### Step 1: View Your Prescriptions
**Option A: From Dashboard Widget**
1. Login to your patient account
2. Look for the **"My Prescriptions"** widget on your dashboard
3. You'll see your 3 most recent prescriptions
4. Click **"View"** to see details or **"Download"** to get the PDF

**Option B: From Prescriptions Page**
1. Navigate to the **"Prescriptions"** section from your menu
2. You'll see all your prescriptions listed

#### Step 2: Download Your Prescription
1. Find the prescription you want
2. Click the **"Download PDF"** button (⬇️ icon)
3. The PDF will be saved to your Downloads folder
4. File name format: `Prescription_YourName_Date.pdf`

#### Step 3: View or Print
- Click **"View PDF"** (📄 icon) to open in a new browser tab
- Click **"Print"** (🖨️ icon) to print directly
- Click **"View Details"** (👁️ icon) to see full information in a modal

## 🔍 Common Questions

### Practitioners

**Q: Can I create a prescription for any patient?**
A: No, you can only create prescriptions for patients with confirmed or completed sessions.

**Q: Can I edit a prescription after creating it?**
A: Currently, you can update the status, notes, and instructions. Contact support for major changes.

**Q: What if a medication is out of stock?**
A: The system checks inventory. If a medication is unavailable, you'll see an error message.

**Q: Can I see prescriptions created by other practitioners?**
A: No, you can only see prescriptions you've created.

### Patients

**Q: When will I receive my prescription?**
A: Your practitioner will create your prescription after your confirmed session. You'll see it immediately in your dashboard.

**Q: Can I download my prescription multiple times?**
A: Yes! You can download and view your prescription as many times as you need.

**Q: What if I can't find my prescription?**
A: Check the status filter - it might be set to "Active" only. Change it to "All Prescriptions" to see everything.

**Q: Is the PDF secure?**
A: Yes, only you can access your prescriptions. They're protected by your login credentials.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### PDF Won't Download
1. Check your browser's download settings
2. Disable popup blockers for CuraOne
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
4. Clear your browser cache

### Can't See Prescriptions
1. Verify you're logged in
2. Check your internet connection
3. Refresh the page
4. Try logging out and back in

### Prescription Not Showing
**For Practitioners:**
- Ensure the session is confirmed or completed
- Check that you submitted the form successfully
- Look in the "All Prescriptions" tab

**For Patients:**
- Your practitioner may not have created it yet
- Check with your practitioner
- Refresh your dashboard
